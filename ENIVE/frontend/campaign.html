GreenGrow: Eco-Friendly Poultry Farm Expansion

Helping sustainable agriculture grow in East Africa by modernizing local poultry farming with solar technology.

About the Business

GreenGrow Poultry started in 2021 with just 50 chickens in a backyard coop. Today, we supply over 20 local restaurants in Nairobi with fresh, organic eggs. Our mission is to provide affordable protein sources while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

Why We Are Raising Funds

We have hit maximum capacity in our current facility. To meet the growing demand, we need to construct a new automated coop that can house 2,000 layers. The funds raised will be used for:

  • Construction of a 500 sqm solar-powered coop.
  • Installation of automated feeding and watering systems to reduce labor costs.
  • Purchase of 1,500 point-of-lay chicks.
  • Marketing and distribution logistics expansion.

Risk Disclosure

Investing in agricultural startups carries risks, including disease outbreaks, market price fluctuation, and weather conditions. We have mitigated these by securing insurance for our livestock and diversifying our feed suppliers.
